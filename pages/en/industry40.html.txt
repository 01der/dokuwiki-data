==== 3-Phase Model of Proceedings how to build an IOT Service from Sensor to Cloud ====
  -Connect.
  -Collect.
  -Consume.
==== Internet-Of-Things Drivers & User Requirements ====
  -Customer centricity: From USP ( Unique Selling Proprosition ) to ISP ( Individual Selling Proposition ).
  -Hyper connectivity.
  -Self-learning products ( -> Algorithms. What may the company's staff member do better than an "algorithm"? ).
  -Realtime big data data processing.
  -Context.
  -Immediate availabilty ( "now" ).
==== Retrofitting of traditional Systems / Machines for Internet-Of-Things ====
  -Access to the control system.
    *Abstraction of the parameters and functions.
    *Implementation of a virtual representation.
  -API-ification of the functions and data.
    *Development of communication technologies.
    *Implementation of online services ( i.e. remote access to system functions ).
  -Networking, accross the automation pyramid, from pyramid to cyber physical system - everybody can talk to anybody.
  -Administration.
    *Backend applications.
    *Business logic.
    *Device / service management
  -GUI applications.
    *Service integration.

==== Internet of Things ( IoT ) ====
  *IoT must satisfy the updated customer's wishes:
    *Reachability of a travel destination ( "Erreichbarkeit eines Reiseziels" ) -> instead  of traditional "Mobility".
    *Availibility ( "Verfügbarkeit" ) -> instead of traditional and antiquated "Brand Loyalty". E.g. The customer might be member of several different car sharing systems.
