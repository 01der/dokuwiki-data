===== Once-free Customer Relationship Management ( CRM ) =====
  *[[http://www.unitedplanet.com/de/|United Planet]].
    *Once there was a free limited CRM software "Intrexx CRM", Microsoft "Windows 2000 Server" or newer ( i.e. W2k Professional, WinXP Professinal is not suitable ), with IIS server, is required for operation. Download or registration is not offered anymore.
      *[[http://www.unitedplanet.com/de/news/44|United Planet "Kostenloses CRM ein Erfolg"]], 2003-05-27.
      *[[http://wayback.archive.org/web/*/http://www.intrexx.com/german/download/downloadcrm.asp|Archive.org "United Planet - Download-Area Intrexx CRM-Studio"]] ( - 2004-06-03 ).
    *Once there was a free limited business portal software "Xtreme Portal Manager", as successor of "Intrexx CRM". Download or registration is not offered anymore.
      *[[http://wayback.archive.org/web/*/http://www.intrexx.com/german/produkte/xtreme_reg.asp|Archive.org "United Planet - Registrierung der Heft-CD Version"]], 2005-10-16.
    *Nowadays, the CRM is just one of many commercial applications for the commercial business portal servers [[http://www.unitedplanet.com/de/intrexx-5-professional|Intrexx Professional]] and [[http://www.unitedplanet.com/de/intrexx-5-compact|Intrexx Compact]].
      *[[http://www.unitedplanet.com/de/application-store/490-Intrexx-CRM|United Planet "Intrexx CRM"]].
      *[[http://www.unitedplanet.com/de/downloads/760|United Planet "CRM Studio - Intrexx Professional / Intrexx Compact RELEASE 5.2]] - PDF manual.
