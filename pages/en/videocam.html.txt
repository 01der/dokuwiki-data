==== Canon MV20i / Canon Elura ====
  *[[http://www.canon.de/|Canon Deutschland]].
  *[[http://www.usa.canon.com/cusa/support/consumer/camcorders/minidv_camcorders/elura|Canon USA "Elura
miniDV Camcorders"]].
  *[[http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/List_of_Canon_camcorders|EN.Wikipedia "List of Canon camcorders"]].
  *Wide-angel converters.
    *Warning: I tested several wide-angel converters with my Canon MV20 video cam, and all failed, as there were shadowed objective corners visible if the video is displayed by a video-processing computer ( not necessarily if the video is displayed on a TV screen, or on the LCD display of the video cam ):
       *Sony VCL 06-37H, 37mm ( little shadowed corners ).
       *Hama 0.45, 37mm ( large shadowed corners, heavy distortion, fisheye view ).
       *Hama 0.5 Digital/44330,37 mm ( large shadowed corner ).
       *Hama 0.45, 46mm ( large shadowed corners ).
       *Kenko KWG-05, 0.5x, 37mm ( large shadowed corners ).
     *I did not yet test these wide-angel converters:
       *JVC GL V 0637U, 37mm.
       *Panasonic VW-LW 4307WE, 0.7x, 43mm.
       *Canon WD46, 46mm ( this is the original device for the Canon MV 20 video cam ).
   *Accumulators.
     *The accumulator [[http://www.troyproducts.de/|Troy Products]] C-L736A with 1800 mAh, [[http://www.varta-consumer.de/|Varta]] V277 with 800 mAh and [[http://www.conrad.de/|Conrad]] #350180-62 with 800 mAh and #355160-62 with 750 mAh are suitable for my Canon MV20 video cam.
   *Windshield.
     *The Rycote Mini Windjammer "[[http://www.rycote.com/|Rycote]] RY-IC2" is suitable as wind screen for the microphone of my Canon MV20 video cam.
   *Tripod.
     *The tripod [[http://www.cullmann.de/|CULLMANN]] "Universal Magic" is the best choice for video filmers ( like me ) who use a bicycle for transportation ! 
     *I was advised to try a "fluid head with balance spring" like the [[http://www.manfrotto.de/|Manfrotto]] #503 to shoot perfect horizontal turns with my DV cam ! Alternatively, with cheap heads, I was advised to hold the head with the left hand and the cam with the right hand, when doing camera turns.

==== Video Recorders ====
  *Cleaning of the rubber pressure wheel ( "Gummi-Andruckkopf" ) - with glass cleaner, which contains no alkohol ( e.g. "Sidolin" ).
  *Cleaning of the video head - with video head cleaner ( "Videokopfreiniger" ), which contains alkohol.
