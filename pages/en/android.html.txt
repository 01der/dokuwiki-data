      -First method:
        -**Start by switching off the device by holding down the Power key for a short while**.
        -**Then press and hold together Volume Up + Power button for a few seconds**.
        -You can release the Power key when Alcatel logo appears on thes screen.
        -Keep  holding the Volume Up button until the Recovery Mode shows up.
        -In the next step use Volume buttons to select "wipe data/factory reset" and tap the Power key to confirm.
        -Afterwards choose "yes" from the menu, and accept it with Power button.
        -When a Recovery Mode appears again on the screen accept option "reboot system now" using Power rocker.
      -Second Method.
        -If the device is off, hold down the Power button to turn it back on.Reset ALCATEL OT-8008D One Touch Scribe HD.
        -Afterwards from Main Menu find and select Settings.
        -Then open the Backup and Reset folder and select Factory Data Reset.
        -Tap Reset Phone and read the warning when it pops up.
        -Choose Erase Everything and the phone will begin restoring.

      -From the options choose Power Off and then tap OK.
      -Wait a couple of seconds. 
      -Finally use the Power button again to turn the phone back on.
    *[[http://www.hardreset.info/devices/alcatel/alcatel-ot-8008d-one-touch-scribe-hd/#recovery-mode|hardreset.info "Recovery Mode ALCATEL OT-8008D One Touch Scribe HD"]].

      -Afterwards press and hold Volume Up + Power button for about 5 seconds.Reset ALCATEL OT-8008D One Touch Scribe HD.
      -You can release the Power key as soon as the Alcatel logo appears on thes screen.Reset ALCATEL OT-8008D One Touch Scribe HD.
      -Keep  holding the Volume Up button until the Recovery Mode shows up. Great job! You are right now in the expected mode.
      -You can navigate here by using the Volume buttons and confirm options by pressing the Power key.
