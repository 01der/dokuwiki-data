===== Theory / Proceedings =====
==== Ten Principles for Opening Up Government Information ====
  *[[http://sunlightfoundation.com/policy/documents/ten-open-data-principles/|Sunlight Foundation "Ten Principles for Opening Up Government Information"]].
    -Completeness.
    -Primacy.
    -Timeliness.
    -Ease of Physical and Electronic Access.
    -Machine readability.
    -Non-discrimination.
    -Use of Commonly Owned Standards.
    -Licensing.
    -Permanence.
    -Usage Costs.
==== The Options for Participation of the People ====
  *[[http://www.eGovernment-Computing.de/|eGovernment Computing]], issue 03-2013, page 6 "Bürgerbeteiligung auf neuen Wegen mit OpenData":
    -Provision of use-oriented informations ( "Nutzungsgerecht aufbereitete Informationen" ).
    -Digital Joint Fact Finding.
    -Linked Open Data.
    -Sustainability and Connectivity.
