====== [hemmerling] My Vision, my Mission, your Benefit ======
===== My Vision =====
To be an approved and fully utilized conultant, who contributes with his services to assure the sustainable prosperity of his customers, small and medium-size companies in Germany.
===== My Mission =====
To accomplish a high and efficient availability, quality, safety and security of services of my customers. I focus on services, to which IT sites and automation sites make an integral contribution.

===== Your Benefit =====
I may be useful, by my planning and consulting, to define, analyse, document, verify, validate and moreover intensify the quality of your IT sites and your automation sites by the implementation of technical and organizational activities. My work is the necessary base and premise to accomplish a high and efficient availability, safety and security.

Rolf Hemmmerling.
