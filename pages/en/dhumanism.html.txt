===== Digital Humanism. 5e - Essence, Engagement, Expression, Experience, Eco ( 2009 ) =====
==== Samsung: 5e - Essence, Engagement, Expression, Experience, Eco ====
  -Essence - Strengthen the Core Value.
  -Engagement - Share your Experience.
  -Expression - Express your own Style / Express Myself.
  -Experience - Use intuitively.
  -Eco - Be environment-friendly.

==== Resources ====
  *IFA 2009, 2009-09-04: [[http://www1.messe-berlin.de/vip8_1/website/Internet/Internet/www.ifa-berlin/englisch/PRESS-SERVICE/Press-Information/index.jsp?newssys_id=29152&newslang=en|Keynote topic: Boo-Keun Yoon (Samsung) calls for "digital humanism"]].

    -Essence: Products’ core values will be strengthened, so they deliver at the highest levels of performance expectation.
    -Engagement: Products will be networkable and interoperable, so that consumers can share content, messages, and their daily experiences with others.
    -Expression: Consumers will have greater choice in color and design, to better reflect personality and environment.
    -Experience: Products will emphasize intuitive interface and ease of use.
    -Eco: Products will reflect consumers’ growing affinity for environmental awareness, emphasizing eco-friendly materials and energy savings.
