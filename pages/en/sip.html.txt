===== SIP Criticism =====
  -There is no status display, whether a person is willing to accept calls, which is a standard feature for Unified Messaging systems ( "Availability" / "Kommunikationsbereitschaftsanzeige" ).
  -There is no way to encrypt communications on ISO Layer 2, where it would be useful. On the other hand, Microsoft with its properitary protocols encrypt communications on ISO Layer 3 ?! There are  3 different ways to encrypt communications, Microsoft choose one of it.
