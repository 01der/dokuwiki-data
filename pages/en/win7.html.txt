==== Activation of the Account "Administrator" on Win7 Home Premium, Vista Business ====
  *[[http://www.askvg.com/how-to-enable-activate-hidden-administrator-account-in-windows-vista/|AskVG.com - Tweaking with Vishal "How to Enable / Activate Hidden Administrator Account in Windows Vista and 7"]].
    -Click on "Start button -> All Programs -> Accessories". 
    -Run Command.com.
      *Right-click on “Command Prompt” and select “Run As Administrator“. If you are prompted to enter password, enter the password and continue. 
      *You can also open Command Prompt in Administrator mode by typing “cmd” in Startmenu Search box and press "Ctrl+Shift+Enter".
    -Now provide following command: //net user administrator /active:yes//
