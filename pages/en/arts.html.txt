===== Graphical Design Rules =====
  -Amorph vs. geometry.
  -Details vs. emptyness.
  -Chaois vs. order.
  -Dark vs. light -> tonal value.
  -Coluor contrasts.
  -Edge direction.
  -Just one determinant focus in an image.
  -Soft vs. hard -> outline.
  -Order is created by symmetry.
  -Repetition of elements ( form languages ).
  -Horizontal vs. vertical.

===== Graphical Character Design Rules ( of Characters for Games... ) =====
  -Characters should not be defined by its extenions.
    *Counterexample for characters defined by extensions: [[http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/The_Smurfs|The Smurfs]].
  -Create "ownerable" design - There is a standard warrior, a senior warrior and finally the boss warrior.
