===== Software & Hardware Design for Automation Industry =====
==== Impact of Hardware Resets ====
  *A hardware reset has negative effect on:
    -Operation-hour counters in PLCs which just store data in PLC's RAM.
    -If you configured your control parameters manually, for several hours ( without hand-written documentation of the data ).
