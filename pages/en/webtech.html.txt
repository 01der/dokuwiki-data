===== Regular Expresssions =====
==== Online Services ====
  *The free online service [[http://www.regex101.com/|regex101 - Online regex tester and debugger: JavaScript, Python, PHP, and PCRE]].
  *Example of [[http://django-marcador.keimlink.de/en/urls_views.html?highlight=regular|django-marcador "8. URLs and Views]]:
    *Python code: <code>from django.conf.urls import url
urlpatterns = [
    url(r'^user/(?P<username>[-\w]+)/$', 'marcador.views.bookmark_user',
        name='marcador_bookmark_user'),
    url(r'^$', 'marcador.views.bookmark_list', name='marcador_bookmark_list'),
]</code>
    *Regex101: The test string <code>user/hemmerling/</code> matches the expression <code>user\/(?P<username>[-\w]+)\/</code>
