===== Design =====
==== Costs of a MES system ====
  -Costs for consulting prior a roll-out.
  -Purchase costs, costs of licenses.
  -Implementation costs
  -Roll-out costs.
  -Operational costs.
==== Customer Benefits of a MES system ====
  -Integrated data transparency.
  -Reduction of required time.
  -Reduction of required administration.
  -Improvement of the customer services.
  -Quality improvement.
  -Early warning system, real time cost control.
  -Increase of the staff productivity.
  -Legal compliance.
==== Software Design Approaches ====
  -Application centric systems.
  -Database centric systems.
    *Prefer systems which have no interfaces between  the MES components, but which have a single data base ( pool, German "Datenbasis" ).
==== Manufacturing Model, to be designed within a MES software ====
  *You can either create one of the models, or both, within your MES software ( Be shure to ask the MES vendor how to do this with the MES tool, or what other model you can design! ):
    -Physical model.
    -Model according to ISA.
==== Manufacturing Processes to be handled by a MES software ====
  -Manufacturing Flow Design.
  -Manufacturing Flow Planning.
  -Manufacturing Flow Execution.
==== The functional Structure of a MES ====
  *According to the book [[http://www.amazon.de/exec/obidos/ASIN/3540343091/hemmerling-21|Jürgen Kletti: "Konzeption und Einführung von MES-Systemen: Zielorientierte Einführungsstrategie mit Wirtschaftlichkeitsbetrachtungen, Fallbeispielen und Checklisten"]], page 61, a MES consist of the components:
    -Production.
    -Quality.
    -Human Resources.
    -Escalation Management.
  *The "Escalation Management" is the linking platform for all other functional modules of a MES. There you can define 	"action control limits" and alarms, by which you can act rapidly in case of violation of limits. By this, the persistance of faulty states can be avoided and so the operating efficiency can be increased.
    *[[http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Escalation|EN.Wikipedia "Escalation"]], [[http://de.wikipedia.org/wiki/Eskalation|DE.Wikipedia "Eskalation"]].
