===== Statements =====
  *[[http://www.linkedin.com/groupAnswers?viewQuestionAndAnswers=&discussionID=142504717&gid=67917&commentID=90490770&trk=view_disc&ut=2Yb13P3tXtTlk1|LinkedIn, Mark Colquhoun]]:
    *"MES systems have one or a combination of the following characteristics:
      -They send operations to a control system to be executed.
      -They read data from the control system as feedback on the state of an operation.
      -They read information from a control system for further analysis by the business. 
    *MES systems often also integrate with enterprise business systems however this is an optional characteristic compared to those listed above".
