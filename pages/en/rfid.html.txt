===== Criticism =====
  *Be Aware:
    *It is most important to find the right transponder, than anything else ( frequency, reader,... ).
    *In general, manufacturers of readers are not experts for transponders.
    *Developers of suitable transponders for your project / application are hard to find, so search worldwide!
