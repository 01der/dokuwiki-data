====== [hemmerling] FHH-Motorsport - The Formula Student Germany (FSG) Team at Fachhochschule Hannover, University of Applied Sciences and Arts ======
===== History and Motivation for this Page =====
I participated in the [[http://www.fhh-motorsport.de/|FHH-Motorsport]], [[http://formula-student.fh-hannover.de/|FHH-Motorsport]] team of [[http://www.fh-hannover.de/|Fachhochschule Hannover, University of Applied Sciences and Arts]], 2007-10-29 - 2008-12-18. As part of my work, I setup this page. Some of the data here is just references to past competitions, but was important during my active membership time. As Alumni of my university, I am not involved in active development anymore.
