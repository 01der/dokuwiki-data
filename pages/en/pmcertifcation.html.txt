=====  GPM/IPMA Level D Certification & GPM Transfernachweis =====
==== Benefits ====
 If you pass the "GPM/IPMA Level D2 certification in Germany, you are granted one year free full membership in the [[http://www.gpm-ipma.de/|GPM]] !

==== What must I learn for Level D from the "PM3" Book  and "Projektmanager" Book ? ====
  *Skills
    -PM3, Kennen # not really important, for level D.
    -PM3, Wissen # **you should know it for Level D**.
    -PM3, Können # **most important  for Level D**.
    -PM3, Managen # not relevant for Level D, but for higher levels C,B,A.
  *Sub-chapters to read
    -PM3, Grundlagenwissen # **most important for Level D**.
    -PM3, Vertiefungswissen # not relevant for Level D, but for higher levels C,B,A.
  *Answering the questions at the end of each chapter of the "Projektmanager" Book # **most important for Level D**.
    *[[http://www.slideshare.net/assessor/projektmanager-fragen-antworten|Slideshare "Guido Hartmann 'ProjektManager Fragen & Antworten'"]].

So if you are trained to write a business plan, e.g. you know how to structure informations in a formal business plan document, you might be the right to write good project planning reports !
