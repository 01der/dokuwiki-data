==== Free IT Administration Tools ====
  *[[http://www.fusiondirectory.org/|Fusion Directory]] - From a simple Web browser, FusionDirectory allows you to set and manage the various components of your information system.
    *User management (Windows / Unix).
    *Management of messaging (transport and storage).
    *Management of workstations (Linux / windows / Thin Client).
    *Management of telephony (Asterisk / Hylafax).
    *Management of network service (SMTP / DNS / DHCP / Samba / CUPS).
    *Access Management.
    *Precise management by delegation of authority.
