====== [hemmerling] Directions - So you'll find me ======
===== With public short-distance transportation =====
  *The tramway stop ( of the tram lines 3, 7, 17 ) next to my domicile is "Schünemannplatz". 
===== With public long-distance transportation =====
  -By plane to [[http://www.hannover-airport.de/|Airport Hannover]].
    *Please ride with the rapid-transit railway "S 5" to the railway station "Hannover-Linden/Fischerhof" ( [[http://reiseauskunft.bahn.de/bin/query.exe/en|Deutsche Bahn, train, tram and bus schedule]] ).
  -By train to the main railway station "Hannover HBF" or to the railway station "Hannover-Linden/Fischerhof" ( [[http://reiseauskunft.bahn.de/bin/query.exe/en|Deutsche Bahn, train, tram and bus schedule]] ).
  -There, please take the tram lines 3 -> direction "Wettbergen", 7 -> direction "Wettbergen" or 17 -> direction "Wallensteinstraße" ( [[http://www.gvh.de/|GVH, tram and bus schedule]] ).
  -In the direction of the tram, heading out of town, "Henckellweg" is the first street on the left side, after the road crossing at which the tram stop is situated. It is labeled with a big blue road sign "[[http://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/File:Zeichen_325.svg|traffic calmed neighbourhood]]". House "2" is the first house on the right side. Appartment "6" is situated in the middle of the house, on the ground floor.
