=== Bench Games Rules, as of 2013-10 ===
  *Multiple Choice. For each multiple choice question, you must select the one best answer from the choices in order to score a correct answer.
  *Usually 40 questions. For each of the questions, you will have 180 seconds to select your answer. 
  *In a few cases the number of questions ( "Listening Skills" -> 20, "Written English" -> 50  ) and the response time differs ( e.g. 60 seconds ).
  *If you take the same test more than four times during a 90-day period, it will be noted on your public transcript. You should not take the same test more than 5 times within a 24 hour period.
  *[[http://brainbench.com/xml/bb/common/consumer/indfaqs.xml|Brainbench, Inc. "Frequently Asked Questions"]].
    *Section "Brainbench Tests and Methodology. What are Brainbench tests like?".
      *The adaptive test engine produces scores that are reflective of the number of questions answered correctly and the difficulty level of those questions. 
      *Brainbench Certifications are measured on a scale of 1 to 5, with 5 being the maximum score. 
      *A score of 2.75 or higher is required for certification by Brainbench, with a score of 4.0 or higher required to be certified at the Master level.
    *Section "What are Job Role Certifications and how are they different from other Brainbench Certifications?"
      *While a skill certification can prove that you are knowledgeable about a specific skill, a job role certifications proves that you have a core set of skills required to perform a specific job.
      *[[http://www.brainbench.com/xml/bb/skillscenter/consumer/jobrolecenter.xml|Brainbench, Inc. "Job Role Center"]].
  *Certification is valid for 3 years. You are now authorized by Brainbench to reference this cerItification on your resume, personal web page, or elsewhere. You may use the appropriate [[http://www.brainbench.com/content/transcript/consumer/viewlogos.do|logo]] however you like.
