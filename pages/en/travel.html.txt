=== Lost Properties ====
  *The lost-property office for properties lost in trains in main railway stations is usually situated at or next to the luggage lockers / luggage service office. Lost items are stored for 1 week, after the items are transfered to Wuppertal, where they are stored for another 2 months ( but it is expensive to get lost property sent back :-( ).
=== Intercity ===
== Intercity-Zuschlag ==
  *Additional tickets for using fast trains ( "Intercity-Zuschlag" ).
    *You may just order and buy such additional tickets at a counter, not online, not at a ticket machine :-(.
    *In opposite to past decades, the price depends on:
      -The distance.
      -If you have a Bahncard25 ( 25% rebate on the additional ticket ) or Bahncard50 ( 50% rebate on the addtional ticket ).
      -Where you buy it, example: 
        -Hannover-Braunschweig, with Bahncard25, additional fee 3 EUR at the counter.
        -Hannover-Braunschweig, additional fee 10% of the price for the standard ticket, or at least 5 EURs.

  *[[http://www.bahn.de/p/view/bahncard/bahnbonus/punkte.shtml|DB Bahn "Punktestand abfragen und Prämienpunkte einlösen"]].

      *However, at a certain date, the free ticket for 2000 points is not applicable for EuroNight (EN) and CityNightLiner ( CNL ) connections :-(.
